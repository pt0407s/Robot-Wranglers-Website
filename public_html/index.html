Welcome to Robot Wranglers

The Robot Wranglers are Legacy Ranch High School's competitive robotics team, participating in BEST Robotics competitions. We're a dedicated group of students who share a passion for engineering, innovation, and teamwork. From designing and building robots to developing marketing strategies and documenting our process, every member plays a vital role in our success.

Throughout the season, our team works tirelessly to bring ideas to life. We brainstorm solutions, build prototypes, test designs, and refine our robot until it performs at its best. Our drivers train for competition day, mastering control under pressure. Beyond technical skills, we develop leadership, problem-solving, time management, and communication abilities that prepare us for future challenges.
